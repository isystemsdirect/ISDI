# Public Claims Guardrails

All public-facing messaging on the ISDI website and OVERSCITE Preview must adhere to these guardrails:

1. **NVIDIA Alignment:** Use prototype-stage or in-development language. Do not claim NVIDIA acceptance, partnership, certification, or sponsorship unless explicitly authorized.
2. **Customer Traction:** Do not imply deployed customer traction or specific revenue milestones unless verified and approved by the Director.
3. **Autonomous Authority:** Do not imply autonomous compliance authority. The system augments human capability; it does not replace human accountability.
4. **Data Privacy:** Ensure it is clear that the OVERSCITE Preview is a demo environment and does not contain live operational data.
