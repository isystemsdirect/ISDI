# ISDI Annual Report Generation

## Description
Summarize key events, financials, and operational highlights for the year.

## Instructions
1. Collect financial summaries, operational milestones, and strategic achievements for the past fiscal year.
2. Structure the annual report to include: Executive Summary, Financial Overview, Operational Highlights, Governance Updates, and Strategic Outlook.
3. Integrate data from various ISDI departments into a cohesive narrative.
4. Apply the ISDI canonical brand voice to the messaging.
5. Draft the complete annual report document for executive review.

## Output Format
- A comprehensive annual report draft in Markdown format.
- A highlight summary sheet for quick executive review.
