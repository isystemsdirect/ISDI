# ISDI Governance Drafting

## Description
Create templates for governance documents; include legal specifications.

## Instructions
1. Review the specific governance document requirement (e.g., new bylaws, board resolution for a specific action).
2. Retrieve the standard ISDI legal templates and specifications.
3. Draft the required corporate governance document adhering to ISDI's canonical terminology and legal requirements.
4. Ensure all necessary clauses for compliance, authority structure, and decision-making are included.
5. Provide the drafted document for human legal review.

## Output Format
- A drafted Markdown or text document of the required governance material.
- Notes on specific legal sections that require expert human review or customization.
