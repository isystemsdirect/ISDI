# ISDI Legal Inventory

## Description
Generate full list of corporate documents; highlight missing, incomplete files.

## Instructions
1. Access the ISDI corporate document repository.
2. Scan for and identify core legal and governance documents including bylaws, board resolutions, articles of incorporation, and meeting minutes.
3. Cross-reference the identified documents against the standard compliance checklist.
4. Compile a comprehensive legal inventory report.
5. Explicitly highlight any missing, incomplete, or expired files in a dedicated section for immediate action.

## Output Format
- A structured Markdown document summarizing the inventory.
- A clear list of found documents with their status.
- A "Missing/Incomplete Documents" section with recommended next steps.
