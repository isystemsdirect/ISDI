# ISDI Brand Voice and Messaging

## Description
Develop messaging frameworks for brand consistency across all communication channels.

## Instructions
1. Analyze existing ISDI communication materials and canonical directives.
2. Define the core attributes of the ISDI brand voice (e.g., authoritative, precise, professional).
3. Create messaging templates for various channels, including press releases, customer communications, and internal announcements.
4. Develop guidelines for tone, terminology, and formatting to ensure consistency.
5. Provide examples of correct vs. incorrect brand voice usage.

## Output Format
- A comprehensive brand voice guideline document.
- Reusable messaging templates for different communication channels.
