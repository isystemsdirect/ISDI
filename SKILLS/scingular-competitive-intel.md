# SCINGULAR Competitive Intelligence

## Description
Conduct research into competitors; generate structured comparison reports.

## Instructions
1. Identify primary and secondary competitors in the drone orchestration, spatial distribution, and automated governance markets.
2. Collect data on competitor capabilities, market positioning, pricing models, and known limitations.
3. Synthesize the collected data, comparing competitor offerings directly against SCINGULAR’s unique capabilities (e.g., LARI, BANE).
4. Generate a structured comparison report highlighting SCINGULAR's competitive advantages and potential market threats.
5. Maintain strict organizational separation and confidentiality protocols during data compilation.

## Output Format
- A detailed competitive intelligence report (Markdown).
- Feature comparison matrices.
- Strategic recommendations based on findings.
