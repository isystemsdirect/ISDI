# SCINGULAR Market Positioning

## Description
Develop go-to-market strategies, positioning statements, and messaging framework.

## Instructions
1. Analyze the SCINGULAR product overview and competitive intelligence reports.
2. Identify target market segments (e.g., enterprise governance, field operations, drone logistics).
3. Draft core positioning statements that emphasize SCINGULAR's unique value proposition (e.g., governed multi-tool development, forensic auditability).
4. Develop a comprehensive messaging framework tailored to different audience personas (technical, executive, operational).
5. Outline high-level go-to-market strategies aligned with the innovation roadmap.

## Output Format
- A structured document detailing market positioning statements.
- A messaging framework segmented by audience.
- High-level go-to-market strategic outline.
