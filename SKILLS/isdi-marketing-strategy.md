# ISDI Marketing Strategy

## Description
Design and structure marketing campaigns, focusing on target audience and ROI.

## Instructions
1. Review overall ISDI business objectives and the latest SCINGULAR product roadmaps.
2. Identify primary target audiences for upcoming campaigns.
3. Design structured marketing campaigns, outlining key messages, channels, and timelines.
4. Estimate required budget and define metrics for measuring Return on Investment (ROI).
5. Ensure all messaging aligns with the canonical ISDI brand voice and compliance standards.

## Output Format
- Comprehensive marketing strategy document.
- Specific campaign plans with timelines and budget estimates.
